Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a sample lasagna recipe. I hope that you enjoy the dish as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Lay pasta sheets on table
  2. Apply and spread paste on pasta
  3. Add cheese on top of paste
  4. Combine
  5. Bake at 450F for 30 minutes
