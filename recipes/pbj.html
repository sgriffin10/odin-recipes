PB&J Recipe

Description

This is a sample PB&J recipe. I hope that you enjoy the dish as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Jam (any kind)
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Toast Bread
  2. Apply and spread the peanut butter on bread
  3. Add plenty of jam on top of peanut butter
  4. Reap the rewards
